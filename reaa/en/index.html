MODULES

Two Core Modules

⌇

Hydrological Survey

Discharge measurement · Uncertainty analysis · Hydro data

ISO 748 Uncertainty

Velocity-area method with per-point uncertainty. International standard compliant.

Real-Time Hydro Data

HRFCO API integration. Water level, discharge monitoring, dam gate markers.

Baseflow Separation

Automated direct/baseflow separation with visualization and export.

H-Q Rating Curves

Stage-discharge relationship analysis and curve generation.

Satellite Imagery

Google Earth Engine for evapotranspiration and watershed analysis.

PDF Reports

Auto-generated measurement reports. Excel import/export.
